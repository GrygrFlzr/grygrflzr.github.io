Quantum Computing

What it is and why it's important

Powered by Qubits

A qubit is a unit of quantum information. While a bit can only be in one state or the other, qubits can be in a superposition of both at the same time!

Not a Replacement

Quantum computers can only have a massive speed advantage over classical computers when doing certain types of calculations.

Fragile

Qubits will return a classical value upon being observed. This means it must not be disturbed by any outside force or the whole system breaks down.
